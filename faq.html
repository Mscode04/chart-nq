Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NEUCART

Important: NEUCART is an Amazon affiliate. All purchases are made through Amazon's secure platform.

1. What is NEUCART's relationship with Amazon?

NEUCART is an official Amazon affiliate. We provide product recommendations and links that direct you to Amazon's website where all transactions are processed. We earn a small commission when you make purchases through our links, at no additional cost to you.

2. How do I place an order?
  1. Browse products on NEUCART
  2. Click on any product to be redirected to Amazon
  3. Complete your purchase on Amazon's secure checkout
3. Who handles shipping and delivery?

All shipping and delivery is handled directly by Amazon. NEUCART does not maintain inventory or handle any part of the fulfillment process.

For shipping questions, visit Amazon's Shipping Information.

4. How do returns work?

All returns must be processed through Amazon's return system. NEUCART cannot process returns directly.

To initiate a return:

  1. Go to Amazon's Returns Center
  2. Select the order you want to return
  3. Follow Amazon's return instructions
5. Who do I contact for customer support?

For product or order-related issues, please contact Amazon directly:

  • Phone: 1800-3000-9009 (Amazon Customer Service)
  • Online: Amazon Help Center

For questions about NEUCART's website or services, contact us at help@neuraq.in

6. Is my payment information secure?

Yes. All payments are processed directly by Amazon using their secure payment systems. NEUCART never sees or has access to your payment information.

7. Do you price match?

As an affiliate, we don't control product pricing. Amazon's pricing policies apply to all purchases. You may check Amazon's current price match policy here.

8. How do I track my order?

Order tracking is available through your Amazon account:

  1. Go to Your Orders
  2. Find your order in the list
  3. Click "Track Package" for shipping updates
