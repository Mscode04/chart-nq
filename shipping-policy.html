Shipping Policy

Last Updated: June 2025

Important Notice: NEUCART is an Amazon affiliate. All shipping is handled by Amazon according to their shipping policies.

1. Amazon Shipping Overview

All products listed on NEUCART are shipped and delivered by Amazon. Amazon offers several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping (typically 3-5 business days)
  • Expedited Shipping (typically 2 business days)
  • One-Day Delivery (where available)

View Amazon's complete shipping information: Amazon.in Shipping Information

2. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs vary depending on:

  • Your location
  • The shipping speed you select
  • Whether the item qualifies for free shipping
  • Your Amazon Prime membership status

Exact shipping costs are displayed during checkout on Amazon's website.

3. Delivery Times

Estimated delivery times are provided by Amazon during checkout and may vary based on:

  • Product availability
  • Shipping destination
  • Shipping method selected
  • Weather conditions or other unforeseen events

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped by Amazon, you can track it:

  1. Go to Your Orders on Amazon
  2. Find your order in the list
  3. Click "Track Package" for detailed shipping information

5. International Shipping

Amazon's international shipping policies apply to orders shipped outside India:

  • Additional customs fees may apply
  • Delivery times may be longer
  • Not all items are eligible for international shipping

6. Our Role

As an Amazon affiliate, NEUCART:

  • Does not handle shipping directly
  • Cannot expedite shipping
  • Cannot change shipping addresses after order placement

For any shipping-related issues, please contact Amazon customer service directly.
